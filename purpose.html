☰ Commonwealth Network

purposes of the website :

Impact.

To provide sound biblical teachings for the members of the universal body of Christ.

Knowledge.

To equip ministers of the gospel with sound biblical knowledge on virtually all subjects covered in the Bible.

Resources.

To provide ministers of the gospel with teaching materials that will help them grow their churches and feed the members of their congregations.

Connectivity

To network with ministers of the gospel from every continent and nation of the world for the growth and expansion of God’s kingdom on the earth.

Interaction

To provide a forum for both ministers and members of the body of Christ to interact with prevailing global issues on how to tackle such from sound biblical perspectives.

Vision.

To give clarity on major Christian, biblical doctrines that have been long misrepresented in the church of Jesus Christ universal.

Training

To furnish ministers with the needed trainings for effective and efficient combating of satanic incursions into the church of Jesus Christ universal.

Resources

To provide the body of Christ with affordable Christian literature such as books, tracts, manuals and others that contain sound biblical teachings to enrich the souls of its members.

Training

To provide online ministerial trainings for upcoming ministers of the gospel of Jesus Christ.

Fellowship

To provide online church services for all who are in need of such globally.
